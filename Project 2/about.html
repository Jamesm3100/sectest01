About Murdoch Group

Your trusted partner in comprehensive security solutions since 2010.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Murdoch Group Security Consulting has grown from a small local security provider to a leading regional security solutions company. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide reliable, effective security solutions that give our clients peace of mind.

Today, we serve clients across Tamahere, Waikato, and surrounding regions, offering comprehensive security solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial properties. Our team of certified security professionals brings decades of combined experience in security system design, installation, and monitoring.

Our Mission

At Murdoch Group, our mission is to provide security solutions that are:

  • Effective: Our systems are designed to provide real protection against security threats.
  • Reliable: We use only the highest quality equipment and maintain rigorous standards for installation and service.
  • Accessible: We believe that quality security should be available to everyone, offering solutions at various price points.
  • Adaptable: Our systems grow with your needs, from simple home security to comprehensive commercial solutions.
